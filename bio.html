Zoe Monosson

I am a Human Factors Engineering major at Tufts University who believes that art and engineering walk hand in hand. This belief erupted from my passion for design, specifically user centered design. Making and creating is the core of my being, especially chair design and carpentry. This was further confirmed after I spent last semester in Copenhagen, Denmark, designing chairs and learning about Scandinavian Design.

My main field of work is in usability testing, but I am also intersted in graphic design and computer science. As well, I have been exploring the intersection of the two in UI design and computer interface design. Most recently, I designed the interface for an iPad that would in theory be used to accompany university campus tours.

I love folding chocolate wrappers into origami hats, artichokes, and being more competitive when playing card games than sports.
